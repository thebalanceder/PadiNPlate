# Costing & Economic Viability

Comprehensive economic analysis for farming decisions.

## Cost Structure

### Per Hectare Cost Breakdown

| Category | Cost (RM/ha) | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| Land preparation | 400-600 | Plowing, puddling |
| Seed | 150-300 | Certified seed |
| Fertilizer | 600-1,500 | Full program |
| Pesticides/Herbicides | 200-500 | Integrated program |
| Labor | 800-1,500 | Family + hired |
| Irrigation/Water | 100-300 | Pump fuel or fees |
| Harvesting | 300-500 | Manual or machine |
| Drying | 100-200 | Sun or mechanical |
| Transport | 50-150 | To mill or storage |
| **Total** | **3,200-5,550** | |

### Per Relong Conversion
- 1 relong = 0.71 hectares
- Multiply hectare costs by 0.71

## Cost Tiers for Solutions

### Tier 1: Minimum Cost
- Basic inputs only
- Highest risk
- May compromise yield

### Tier 2: Recommended
- Balanced approach
- Moderate investment
- Good yield protection

### Tier 3: Premium
- Full program
- Higher investment
- Maximum yield potential

## Expected Returns

### Yield Calculations
| Input Level | Expected Yield (t/ha) | Gross Revenue (RM) |
|-------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Low input | 3-4 | 6,000-8,000 |
| Medium input | 5-6 | 10,000-12,000 |
| High input | 7-8 | 14,000-16,000 |

*Based on paddy price of RM 2,000/ton*

### Profit Margins
- Net profit = Gross revenue - Total costs
- ROI = (Net profit / Total costs) × 100

## Break-Even Analysis

### Major Investments

#### Water Pump
- Cost: RM 3,000-10,000
- Annual savings on irrigation labor: RM 500-1,000
- Break-even: 5-10 years

#### Mechanical Sprayer
- Cost: RM 500-2,000
- Labor savings: RM 200-500/year
- Break-even: 3-5 years

#### Combine Harvester
- Cost: RM 50,000-150,000
- Custom hiring income: RM 5,000-15,000/year
- Break-even: 10-15 years
- Best via cooperative ownership
