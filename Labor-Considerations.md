# Labor Considerations

Padi farming is labor-intensive. All recommendations should include labor analysis.

## Labor Requirements by Activity

| Activity | Man-days/Hectare | Peak Period |
|----------|------------------|-------------|
| Land preparation | 15-20 | Pre-planting |
| Nursery preparation | 5-8 | 3-4 weeks before transplant |
| Transplanting | 25-35 | Planting |
| Weeding (manual) | 20-30 | 21-45 DAP |
| Fertilizer application | 3-5 | Variable |
| Pesticide application | 3-5 | Variable |
| Irrigation management | 5-10 | Continuous |
| Harvesting | 15-25 | Harvest |
| Threshing | 5-10 | Harvest |
| Drying & storage | 5-8 | Post-harvest |
| Transport | 2-5 | Planting & harvest |

**Total: 83-141 man-days per hectare per season**

## Labor Assumptions

### Family Labor
- Default assumption: 2 family members available
- Family labor cost = 0 (implicit cost)
- May not be sustainable for larger farms

### Hired Labor
- Daily wage: RM 40-60
- Availability: Often scarce during peak periods
- Skills: Experienced workers command premium

## Mechanization Alternatives

### High-Labor Activities to Mechanize
1. **Land preparation** → Power tiller/tractor
2. **Transplanting** → Mechanical transplanter
3. **Harvesting** → Combine harvester
4. **Spraying** → Motorized knapsack/tractor sprayer

### Cost-Benefit of Mechanization
- Compare hiring labor vs. owning/renting equipment
- Seasonal vs. year-round use
- Cooperative ownership models

## Labor-Saving Technologies

| Technology | Labor Saved | Investment Required |
|------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Mechanical transplanter | 20-25 man-days/ha | RM 25,000-50,000 |
| Drum seeder | 10-15 man-days/ha | RM 500-1,000 |
| Cono weeder | 10-15 man-days/ha | RM 300-500 |
| Motorized sprayer | 1-2 man-days/ha | RM 200-500 |
| Combine harvester | 15-25 man-days/ha | RM 80,000-150,000 |

## Labor Shortage Solutions

### For Small Farms
- Labor exchange with neighbors
- Community work groups
- Youth engagement programs

### For Large Farms
- Contract labor teams
- Mechanization
- Contract farming arrangements
