# Stakeholder Integration

Supporting the agricultural extension ecosystem, not replacing it.

## Key Stakeholders

### 1. Agricultural Extension Officers
- Government extension staff
- Private company agronomists
- NGO field workers
- Farmer organization leaders

### AI's Role with Extension Officers
- Decision support tool, not replacement
- Help prioritize farm visits
- Provide data for better advice
- Scale extension reach

### 2. Farmers' Organizations
- Farmers' Association (Pertubuhan Peladang)
- KADA (Kedah) / MADA (Kedah)
- Local cooperatives
- Water user associations

### 3. Government Agencies
- Ministry of Agriculture
- Malaysian Agricultural Research and Development Institute (MARDI)
- Department of Agriculture
- State agricultural departments

### 4. Input Suppliers
- Fertilizer companies
- Seed companies
- Agrochemical companies
- Equipment dealers

### 5. Financial Institutions
- Banks with agricultural loans
- Agribusiness financing
- Government subsidies disbursement

### 6. Market Intermediaries
- Rice millers
- Collectors/traders
- Wholesalers
- Retailers

## Integration Points

### Government Subsidies & Programs
| Program | Description | Eligibility |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Subsidi Baja | Fertilizer subsidy | Registered farmers |
| Skim Pemberian Benih | Seed assistance | Smallholders |
| Skim Pengairan | Irrigation support | Area-based |
| Skim Risiko Persaraan | Crop insurance | Voluntary |

### How AI Can Help
- Inform farmers of eligible programs
- Guide application process
- Track program benefits received

### Extension Officer Dashboard
- View farmer questions in area
- AI-prepped recommendations
- Success stories and case studies
- Training materials

## Feedback Loop

### Continuous Improvement
1. Farmers report recommendation accuracy
2. Extension officers verify outcomes
3. Researchers analyze patterns
4. AI models updated regularly
5. Better recommendations generated

### Success Metrics
- Adoption rate
- Recommendation accuracy
- Yield improvement
- Cost savings
- Farmer satisfaction
